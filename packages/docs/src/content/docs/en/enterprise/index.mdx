## Overview

Enterprise components are designed to visualize and manage complex business processes. These components are built on top of the standard component library and offer additional features.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Process Management" icon="setting">
    ProcessFlow, WorkflowDesigner, ApprovalChain — workflows and approval processes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risk & Compliance" icon="warning">
    RiskMatrix, AuditLog, ComplianceDashboard — risk assessment and compliance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data Analysis" icon="document">
    Kanban, Scheduler, FormBuilder — data-driven business applications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reporting" icon="information">
    ReportBuilder, ChartComposer, DashboardLayout — enterprise reporting.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
